# Manufacturing Checklist

## Before Manufacturing

- [ ] Latest drawing released
- [ ] Material and specification confirmed
- [ ] Stock dimensions checked
- [ ] Tooling and fixtures available
- [ ] Critical dimensions identified
- [ ] Inspection method agreed

## During Manufacturing

- [ ] Workholding secure
- [ ] First operation inspected
- [ ] Heat-affected areas controlled
- [ ] Threads protected or chased
- [ ] Sharp edges deburred
- [ ] Part marked or labeled

## After Manufacturing

- [ ] Critical dimensions measured
- [ ] Surface finish acceptable
- [ ] Fastener fit checked
- [ ] Assembly fit checked
- [ ] Mass recorded
- [ ] Photos uploaded
- [ ] Drawing marked complete
